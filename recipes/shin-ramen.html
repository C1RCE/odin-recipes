Shin Ramen

Description

Shin is a Korean brand of spicy instant ramen noodles. The dish is garnished with spring onions and an egg.

Ingredients

  • 500ml water
  • 1 packet of Shin ramen
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add ramen and empty seasoning and dried vegetable packets to the water.
  3. Stir after 2 minutes.
  4. After another minute, crack egg into the pot and cover for 30 seconds.
  5. Serve immediately.
