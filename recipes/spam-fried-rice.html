Spam Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice with eggs, diced vegetables and spam.

Ingredients

This recipe serves 4 people.

  • 1 can of diced spam
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/4 cup diced carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup thawed frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. In large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil.
  2. Add eggs. Cook, stirring to desired doneness. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  3. In same skillet, heat remaining 1 tablespoon oil. Cook spam, carrots, green onions, and bell pepper for 4 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in rice and egg. Sprinkle with soy sauce. Heat thoroughly.
  5. Garnish as desired with additional green onion.
